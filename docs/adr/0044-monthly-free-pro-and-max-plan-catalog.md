# Monthly Free Pro And Max Plan Catalog

Tab offers Free, Pro, and Max. This ADR supersedes ADR-0043's Free-and-Pro-only catalog, prices, and decision to defer Max. ADR-0043's local-first architecture, value-based metering rules, Personal Memory boundary, entitlement enforcement, and implementation policies remain in effect.

Free remains unchanged: 100 Accepted Words from Local Suggestions per day, 10 successful Deep Completes per month, one Mac, and management of existing Personal Memory. Every new account still receives one 30-day account-level Pro trial without a payment card; reinstalling or linking another Mac does not restart it.

Pro costs $10 per month and includes unlimited Accepted Words, 300 successful Deep Completes per month, continuous Memory Extraction, custom writing instructions, the supported model catalog, and up to three personal Macs. Max costs $20 per month and includes 1,000 successful Deep Completes per month. Max otherwise has the same capabilities and three-device limit as Pro.

Paid plans are monthly only. Tab does not offer an annual plan and has no annual customers to migrate or grandfather. Checkout therefore selects only a plan; the billing interval is fixed to monthly. User-facing subscription copy should remain plan-neutral where it applies to both Pro and Max, while account status should render the specific plan returned by the entitlement authority.

The three-plan catalog gives occasional, regular, and heavy Deep Complete users explicit choices without differentiating local writing, personalization, model access, or device count between paid tiers. Prices and allowances remain hypotheses to validate against retained usage, conversion, and cloud gross margin.
